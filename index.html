Boot·strapping - a self-sustaining process that proceeds without external help.

Bootstrapd is a community gathering for software craftspeople who are---or aspire to be---building and selling their own products.

We don't want VC funding, we aren't looking for a big exit; we want to create a sustainable living enjoying our craft.
